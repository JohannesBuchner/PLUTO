Computes the volume average of staggered magnetic fields to return cell-centred values. bp: B component at right staggered face (e.g. Bx[i, j, k]) bm: B component at left staggered face (e.g. Bx[i-1, j, k]) dir: direction of staggered. options: IDIR, JDIR, KDIR l: integer for cell along direction of staggered. Grid: PLUTO grid structure. return: average field.
