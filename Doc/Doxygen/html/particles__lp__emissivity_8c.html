double  freq_GHz,
double  ThObs,
double *  JnuIC

Estimates the power emitted by an ensemble of electron N(E) with Isotropic radiation field of CMB photons. For non-relativistic flow approximations in the Thompson's limit are taken from Petruk et. al. 2009, typically used for Supernovae. For the relativistic flow approximations in the Thompson's limit used here are adopted from Dermer et. al. 1995. The relativistic implementation therefore account of beaming of incident CMB photon and also related relativistic effects.

Parameters
[in]plPointer to the Particle Structure.
[in]freq_GHzObserved frequency of InverseCompton radiation in GHz.
[in]ThObsLine of Sight Angle subtended from the Z axis in degrees.
[out]JnuICTotal InverseCompton emissivity at the prescribed frequency
