14  * Quantities are evaluated at zone face, NOT edge.
15  *
16  * The electric field components are given by
17  * the fluxes of the induction equation:
18  *
19  * +----+
20  * | 0 |
21  * |-Ezi| at x - faces

23  * +----+

31  * +----+

33  * | Exk| at z - faces
34  * | 0 |
35  * +----+
36  *
37  * Only the components required to update the normal megnetic
38  * field are required.
39  * For example, at the lower x-boundary, the necessary
40  * electric field components to update bx are Ez and Ey (no Ex).
41  * Thus one needs to specify
